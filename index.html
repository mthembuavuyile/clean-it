About Clean.It

Clean.It Recycling and Waste Management was founded by two young, visionary entrepreneurs committed to turning waste into valuable resources. Our mission is to drive sustainability, energy solutions, and job creation by collaborating with businesses, schools, and communities.

Vision

To create a cleaner, sustainable future by transforming waste into opportunities that drive job creation and environmental stewardship across Africa.

Mission

We aim to reduce waste, promote sustainable energy solutions, and create jobs by engaging communities, businesses, and schools in responsible waste management practices.
