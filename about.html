About Us

Clean.It Recycling and Waste Management was founded by two young entrepreneurs passionate about reducing waste and promoting sustainability in South Africa. We aim to create value from waste by working with businesses, schools, and communities to adopt better recycling and waste management practices.

Our focus is on practical, everyday solutions that contribute to a cleaner, more sustainable environment. By providing accessible recycling services and engaging with local communities, we are building a foundation for positive change—one step at a time.

As we grow, we aim to collaborate with more partners to promote responsible waste practices that benefit both the environment and local economies.

Vision

Our vision is to help create a more sustainable future in South Africa by reducing waste and promoting recycling practices. We aim to empower local communities with the tools and knowledge they need to make a positive environmental impact.

Mission

Our mission is to:

  • Promote responsible waste management and recycling practices in South Africa.
  • Engage local communities, businesses, and schools in sustainability efforts.
  • Work with partners to create practical solutions that make recycling more accessible and effective.
  • Drive job creation and economic opportunities through sustainable waste management projects.
